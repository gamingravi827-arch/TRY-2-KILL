Our Legacy

Founded in 2023, TRR TO KILL has risen from the ashes to become one of the most feared squads in the region.

The Mission

T2K was built on a simple principle: Domination through Unity. We are not just a guild; we are a family of elite players who strive for perfection in every match. Whether it's classic BR or Clash Squad, we play to win.

Our goal is to foster a competitive environment where players can grow, learn advanced strategies, and participate in high-stakes tournaments.

Guild Rules

01

Respect All

Respect fellow members and opponents. Toxicity is zero tolerance.

02

Be Active

Daily glory contribution is mandatory. Inactivity for 3 days = Kick.

03

Change Name

All members must use the T2K tag within 7 days of joining.

04

Mic On

Communication is key. Mic usage is required during squad games.

05

Loyalty

Loyalty to the guild is paramount. Multi-guilding is not allowed.

06

Scrims

Participation in scheduled scrims and tournaments is highly encouraged.
